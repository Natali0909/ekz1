An Egyptian paradise at your fingertips

Barceló Tiran Sharm**** is a luxury hotel located in the northern part of Sharm El Sheikh, on the coast of Nabq, with spectacular views of the Gulf of Aqaba and the island of Tiran.

The hotel occupies an area of 80,000 square metres and is located in front of a beautiful private beach covering 1100 metres which boasts crystal clear waters of the amazing coral reef.

The hotel offers 345 rooms of different categories: doubles, family rooms and suites. All have their own balcony from which to enjoy views over the sea and the gardens.

Why choose Barceló Tiran Sharm

  • In the exclusive area of Sharm El Sheikh
  • Private beach on the Red Sea
  • Ideal for diving lovers
  • Pool area with views and swim-up rooms
